About Toto

Toto was more than just a puppy – he was the soul that lit up my childhood,
the silent friend who stood by me through laughter, tears, and every season
of life. Though small in size, he carried a heart larger than life, filled
with joy, curiosity, boundless energy, and a kind of unconditional love that
few ever experience, even among humans. He came into my life when I was just
9 years old, and from that moment on, every day with him was a lesson in loyalty,
gentleness, and true companionship.

Toto wasn’t just a dog – he was my child, my little brother, my quiet
support, and a piece of heaven sent to earth. He taught me to love without
expectations, to find joy in the simple things, and to be present with my
whole heart. And when his final moment came, I was there, holding him close,
just as he had always stayed close to me in my hardest times.

Though he is no longer with me in body, I feel his gentle and playful
spirit forever woven into who I am. My dear Toto, you may have left this world,
but you will forever remain in my heart – where true love never dies.
